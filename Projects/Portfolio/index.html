About

The repository of web apps in the portfolis above, are developed by the class of 2025 in Fullstack Web Development training conducted by an experienced trainer with vast experienced in Javascript, HTML, CSS, React, Express, Node and MongoDB.

The advantage of this training program is the daily engagement with candidates, small classes, and peer pair-programing. We develop real world web apps and collaborate amongst the students which mimicks an environment of real workplace Development teams.
